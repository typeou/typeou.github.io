Terms of Service

Pricing

  • Payment will be accepted through Square in Canadian Dollars (CAD).
  • USD prices as listed are based on the current CAD-to-USD conversion rounded up to the nearest $5 USD.
    • This conversion is peformed automatically based on the latest data from the Bank of Canada.
  • After the final price is decided, an invoice will be sent to the client's email through Square.
  • Please note that the price listed on the invoice will be in Canadian Dollars (CAD).
  • Upon receipt of the invoice, the client must pay at least 50% of the listed price before work will begin.
    • If the client wishes, they may choose to pay 100% of the fee upfront.
  • Work will not begin until at least 50% of payment has been received.
  • Finished model will not be sent until 100% of payment has been received.
  • Prices assume commercial use rights.

Process

  • Turnaround time will vary depending on complexity and other factors.
    • Typical turnaround time is 1-3 weeks from the time work begins on the model.
  • Once work has started, refunds will not be provided.
    • If work does not begin immediately after payment, I will contact you before I start.
  • I reserve the right to decline a commission for any reason, at any point in the process.
    • A full refund, if applicable, will be provided if I cancel a commission at any point in the process.
  • I reserve the right to display the finished model on my page(s). Credit will be given.
