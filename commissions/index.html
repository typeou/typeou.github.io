Welcome to my commission page! I do rigging-only commissions for Live2D models intended for use by VTubers/VStreamers.

If you are interested, please have a model in ready-to-rig form. The model should be in .psd format and separated in a way similar to that shown in this video by Emma A. from Iron Vertex.

I can make some minor adjustments to suit my needs if I find it necessary, but I do not offer model splitting services.

I do humanoid rigs! Large deviation from this (furry, mech, etc) is a no-go, unfortunately.
