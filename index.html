Hello, and welcome to my website! For now, its main purpose is to serve as an information hub for my Live2D rigging commissions.

Eventually, I'll probably add a dev blog of sorts for projects I'm working on or have worked on in the past.

I'm probably supposed to mention some things about me. I'm a developer, mainly; in the process of finishing up my bachelor's degree in Computer Science. The focus of my studies in CS has leaned towards the creative side of things, given my end goal of being a game dev. I have a lot of personal experience in Unity development as well as some professional experience in full-stack web development.

Other than that, I try to build my other creative skills as well. I can do some half-decent digital art if I try really hard, some okay character design, 3D modelling, Live2D rigging, writing... I'm pretty useless when it comes to any kind of audio production, though. No idea how to make music at all. I should really get on that.

Anyway, if you're here to inquire about a commission, feel free to check out the commissions page! It should have most of what you need to know. If you're not here for that, I'm honestly not sure what you're here for right now. But, hey! Thanks for visiting. I hope you're having a good day.

Stay safe! -typeou

Welcome to my commission page! I do rigging-only commissions for Live2D VTuber models.

If you are interested, please read the Terms of Service and then click here to submit a form.

Please have a model in ready-to-rig form. The model should be in .psd format and separated in a way similar to that shown in this video by Enma A. from Iron Vertex.

I can make some minor adjustments to suit my needs if I find it necessary, but I do not offer model splitting services.

I also do chibi rigs! These are significantly simpler rigs, a great option for newer or more budget-oriented VTubers. Due to their simple nature, the turnaround time is also likely to be quicker.

A chibi rig has all the standard features of a rig, excluding head and body X/Y rotations. These options can be optionally be included as an add-on!

Partnered Model Artist

If you'd like a rig from me but are in need of a model artist, I highly recommend b00lien.

We've worked together ever since my first rig, and are literally feet away from each other, so we'll be able to seamlessly communicate back-and-forth in order to provide you with the smoothest modelling and rigging process possible. We can discuss in real time how the model needs to be split and any changes that need to be made during the process in order to ensure a high quality and timely delivery.

Of course, I'm still fully willing to communicate about and work with models from other artists! I'll do my best to provide you with a quality product, no matter what.

Pricing

  • Payment will be primarily accepted through Square in Canadian Dollars (CAD).
    • Please note that payment made through Square does not require an account.
    • Square accepts card, Google Pay, and Apple Pay.
    • Optionally, I can also accept payment through PayPal. However, I greatly prefer Square.
  • USD prices as listed are based on the current CAD-to-USD conversion rounded up to the nearest $5 USD.
    • This conversion is peformed automatically based on the latest data from the Bank of Canada.
  • After the final price is decided, an invoice will be sent to the client through the relevant service.
  • Please note that, if payment is being processed through Square, the price listed on the invoice will be in Canadian Dollars (CAD).
    • The CAD-to-USD (or other relevant currency) conversion, as of the time of sending, will be listed in the invoice notes.
  • For commissions $60 (CAD) and under, 100% of the payment is required before work will begin.
  • For commissions over $60 (CAD), at least 50% of the payment is required before work will begin.
    • If the client wishes, they may choose to pay 100% of the payment upfront.
  • Finished product will not be sent until 100% of payment has been received.
  • Prices assume commercial use rights.

Process

  • Turnaround time will vary depending on complexity and other factors.
    • Typical turnaround time is 1-3 weeks from the time work begins.
    • For rush services, please contact. Please note a surcharge of 50% of the base price.
  • Commissions are not necessarily first-come-first-serve.
  • Once work has started, refunds will not be provided.
    • If work does not begin immediately after payment, I will contact you before I start.
  • I reserve the right to decline a commission for any reason, at any point in the process.
    • A full refund, if applicable, will be provided if I cancel a commission at any point in the process.
  • I reserve the right to display the finished product on my page(s). Credit will be given.
