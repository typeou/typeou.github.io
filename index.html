Options

There are two types of stinger transitions I can create: "Classic" and "Track Matte" Stingers.

A Classic Stinger is probably what you're used to seeing. A graphic covers the entire screen for a moment, during which the scene switches. This hides the transition point, resulting in a clean switch between scenes.
A Track Matte Stinger is more complicated, but more powerful. This type of transition doesn't require the entire screen to be covered for the transition to take place, instead it's able to display both scenes during the transition to create more interesting transition effects.

As of the time of writing, Track Matte Stingers are only available on OBS Studio. Streamlabs OBS does not support Track Matte Stingers.
