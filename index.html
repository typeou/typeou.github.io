Hello and welcome to my website! For now its main purpose is to serve as an information hub for my Live2D rigging commissions.

Eventually, it will probably have a dev blog of sorts for projects I'm working on or have worked on in the past.

I'm a recent computer science graduate who concentrated their studies on the creative applications of programming.

As such, my primary skills are in programming and game development! I have a bunch of minor skills like 3D modelling and basic animation, but I'm still a novice at those.

I have a lot of personal experience in Unity development as well as some professional experience in full-stack web development using .NET.

Sometimes I make little tools like KBonk, a viewer interaction tool for VTubers that allows their audience to throw stuff at them.

I've been working on on-and-off on a yet-unannounced project for the past few years. More information on that when it's done.

Anyway, if you're here to inquire about a commission, feel free to check out the commissions page! It should have most of what you need to know. If you're here for help with KBonk, go here! If you're not here for either of those, I'm honestly not sure what you're here for right now. But, hey! Thanks for visiting. I hope you're having a good day.
